I've collaborated with Slack on a wide range of projects, including environmental design for various events. Here's a quick sampling at some of the largest projects we've done together.

A regal and Gatsby-esque brand system for Slack's second anniversary celebration, under the theme of the Roarin' 20s / Great Gatsby. Includes logo options, a mural, branded gifts and signage, etc.

Slack Turns Three

An explosion of color and paint for Slack's third anniversary celebration, held at the Exploratorium in San Francisco. Project includes signage / wayfinding, displays, murals, etc.

Fun & playful signage, graphics, and wayfinding displays for Slack's annual summer picnic.

A branded ice cream truck for the Slack float in the 2016 San Francisco Pride Parade.
