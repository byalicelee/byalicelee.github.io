Wealthfront

Building an illustrative brand

Wealthfront is a financial services product that helps consumers make crucial investment decisions. We worked together to build out their illustration brand system and create 60+ illustrations for the product.

As a tech company within the financial industry, Wealthfront wanted to position itself as a more relatable and human option to large institutions, but still connote the trustworthiness of a financial institution.
